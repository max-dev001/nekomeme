Our Vision

Neko Meme introduces a fresh breeze into the world of meme coins with its cat-themed design. There are no initial token holders. By distributing a total supply of 1 quadrillion tokens through giveaways and airdrops, we aim to achieve a true DAO. As a project completely led by the community, Neko Meme grows together with its users and establishes new standards for cryptocurrencies.

Features

  • Distributing a total of 1 quadrillion NEKO through giveaways and airdrops.
  • NEKO has no owners and aims to operate entirely as a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).
  • Tokens received from airdrops can be unlocked by purchasing on DEXs (e.g., PancakeSwap).

Roadmap
